Connect Spotify

Song previews are powered by Spotify. A free account is all you need — no Premium required.

Quick Setup (2 min)
  1. 1
    Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and sign in with your free Spotify account.
  2. 2
    Click Create App. Give it any name. In Redirect URIs, add exactly: http://localhost:8080 — then save.
  3. 3
    Copy your Client ID from the app's Settings page and paste it above.
  4. 4
    Serve this file locally. In your terminal, run:
    python3 -m http.server 8080
    then open http://localhost:8080/tip-of-your-tongue.html
🔒 Privacy: Your Client ID and token stay in your browser's session only. Nothing is sent to any external server.
